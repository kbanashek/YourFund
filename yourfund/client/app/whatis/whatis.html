What Is Your Fund?

Your Fund is tailored to YOUR goals, YOUR timeframe, and YOUR risk tolerance.

Using an innovative patented process, Your Fund lets you design, create, and manage a customized fund of stocks, ETFs, bonds, or mutual funds. You can easily adjust Your Fund based upon your changing needs.

You can create more than one fund for each of your goals.

Your Fund is also a financial education tool for investors who want to learn about investing, but don’t know where to turn. There are no monthly or annual fees, and low commissions.

With Your Fund, you can:

  • DESIGN and create one or more personalized, customized funds geared for investment goals such as retirement, saving for college, etc.

  • MONITOR Your Fund’s history and performance.

  • RESEARCH stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and ETFs.

  • LEARN about investment concepts, terms and strategies as well as how to analyze a company’s financial statements.

  • INTERACT with other investors on the Information Exchange forum to discuss strategies and investments.
