Why Choose Your Fund?

Your Fund is an affordable way to design and create a customized, diversified fund and learn about investing concepts, techniques, and strategies. It is easy to use and intuitive. Your Fund empowers you with the knowledge to make smart investment decisions and better manage your investments.

Your Fund lets you:

  • create an inexpensive, diversified investment portfolio – brokers do not give you this advantage.
  • control the tax consequences of your capital gains and losses – mutual funds do not give you this advantage.
  • choose the securities that you want – mutual funds do not.
  • purchase small amounts of securities and fractional shares.
  • choose your investments as a portfolio – brokers do not.

Your Fund has:

  • much lower commissions than full service brokers and most discount brokers.
  • NO monthly or annual fees.
  • NO minimum deposit or balance.

Your Fund is a one-stop, comprehensive site providing:

  • Financial education.
  • In-depth information about companies you want to research.
  • An Information Exchange where you can ask investment questions and learn what investment strategies work for other investors.
