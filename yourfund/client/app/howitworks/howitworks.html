How It Works

Simply sign up to open an account, transfer funds into your account, and youâ€™re ready to create your fund. Then follow these simple steps:

  • Go to "Manage Your Fund", determine an amount you want to invest, and then name your fund.

  • Research and choose your investments using the patented Your Fund system.

  • Select your desired percentage for each investment and click "Create".

That's all there is to it.

You can balance the investments in Your Fund to best suit your needs.

You can change the percentages any time you like, add other investments, or sell your investments.
